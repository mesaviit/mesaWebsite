ABOUT US

Established in the year 2013 the Mechanical Engineering Students Association (MESA) is an origination formed by a group of enthusiastic,diligent and creative group of students to develop a comprehensive experience of professional life along with grooming their personality by technical and non technical knowledge.

WHO ARE WE?

MESA is the most active students organization in VIIT,Pune.Every Mechanical Engineering student beginning with the second year in the program at VIIT is a member of MESA. Mentored by experienced faculty members of the Mechanical Engineering department, students take upon many initiatives that prepare them to face the challenges of the future by developing interpersonal skills .We take an active role in organizing both technical and non-technical events and different workshops which are helpful for students.

    MESA always strives for Industry-Institution Interaction, Guiding students for Research papers and Patents,help finding Internships and scholarships, helps to build new connections in your favourite field,arrange workshops and webinars to learn new softwares and skills which helps in achieving all round development of the student. In addition to technical events, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities are also organized by MESA all around the year. Functions such as Engineer's Day, Teachers day,B.Tech Farewell,Mechmerize and Model United Nations are organized by MESA.All these activities helps our members to understand their forte of working and learn new skills.MESA has its own official committee headed by President, Ladies Representative, secretary and other domain Heads and associates,which makes us most active and professional club of our college.
